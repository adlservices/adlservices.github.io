2. Next, download this IPA file on your phone:

If it comes up with Untrusted Developer warning, go to Settings, then General and scroll down to Device Management then trust the developer.

3. Open AltStore on your phone and go to the apps tab. Click on the plus button and find your mySAGOV.ipa file, then tap it. It should start installing.

Make sure your iphone is still connected to your mac and Altserver is running.

4. The app should then give you a code. Send me your code and I'll unlock it remotely, bump messages if I don't reply. You should now be able to unlock the app.

5. Click this link and enter the relevant details, make sure to follow the examples. Your Card ID Number can be your real one if you want.

Next, press click to confirm details and then download your config file.

6. Now go to your 'Downloads' folder in Files and copy the file called 'config.json'. Rename it if you have to. Then go out and paste the 'config.json' file into the 'mySAGOV' folder.

7. Find a photo for your ID photo. It's best if it's a portrait photo of you with a white background, 500x500 pixels. In the camera roll, share the photo and 'Save to Files'. Save it to the 'mySAGOV' folder then rename it as 'portrait.jpg'.

8. Relaunch the app and everything should be good! Swipe up the MySAGOV app then reopen it.

Also, because of Apple's regulations, the app has to be refreshed every 7 days. You can simply do this by reconnecting your phone to your computer and reinstalling Altstore through Altserver on your computer. Takes like 5 mins.
